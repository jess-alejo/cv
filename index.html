About My Career

Life is unpredictable. We never know what's ahead of us. I never thought I would be a programmer until opportunity knocks.

I completed my BS in Computer Science degree through a scholarship. I didn't immediately get a job after graduation, and my first computer-related career does little to no coding work.

I started my professional programming career late around 2011. It is then when I realize how much less I know, so I crave to learn more.

In the beginning

My interest in programming sparkled when I saw my name appears in a Visual FoxPro message box. Since then, I became eager to learn to program and feed my curiosity about computers and technology.

Years have passed, and so many things have changed. Likewise, technology changes so rapidly. I have also discovered other programming languages, but I love C# and Ruby at most. Reminiscing the past, I believe that that very first fantastic moment is the most crucial experience in my career.
